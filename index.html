Meet Chef Isra Primitivo

Born in the highlands of Costa Rica, Chef Isra discovered his passion for cooking through his grandmother's traditional recipes and ancient fire cooking methods passed down through generations.

After training in prestigious kitchens across Europe and returning to his roots, Isra founded Primitivo to share the authentic flavors and techniques of his homeland with international visitors seeking genuine culinary experiences.

Every dish tells a story of Costa Rica's rich culinary heritage, prepared with locally sourced ingredients and cooked using traditional fire techniques from the region.
